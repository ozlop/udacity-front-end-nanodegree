More About Me

My name is Oswaldo (Ozzy) Lopez and I am a full-time father and full-time learner; tech-wizard in the making.

After embarking on my life-long journey of discovery in programming in 2017, from the back-end to the front-end, top to bottom, the ambition and yearning to learn I have cultivated will remain my driving force for many years to come.

My desire to further my understanding of modern technologies has led me into the domain of web development where I have found myself enamored with full stack development processes. This has led me to pursue avenues of personal development that involve working on personal projects and creating personal web pages and/or applications for friends, family and anyone who has given me the chance to do so.

I aspire to leverage my findings in order to implement solutions to everyday problems that will enable people to reach an optimized level of productivity in business and in personal life (and hopefully make a buck or two in the process.)
